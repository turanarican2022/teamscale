Frequently Asked Questions

We tried to answer most common questions, if you have any additional, please get in touch with our friendly team

01. What are properties in Figma?

In Figma, properties refer to the characteristics or attributes of design elements, such as layers, shapes, or text. These properties can include visual aspects like size, color, position, and more, which designers can adjust to create and customize their designs.

02. How Whitespace UI kit and Design System can help me?

The main feature of Whitespace UI that it saves you time. If it saves you time, it automatically saves you money. You no longer have to create a new UI kit from scratch before starting each project. Use Whitespace UI on an unlimited number of projects and save hundreds of hours and thousands of dollars.

03. What is your return policy?

For Figma designs, as digital products are non-tangible and can be instantly accessed upon purchase, we don't offer returns or refunds. However, if you encounter any issues or have questions about your purchase, please contact us, and we'll do our best to assist you.

04. How can I contact customer support?

You can reach our customer support by emailing support@teamscale.io or filling out the contact form on our website. We aim to respond promptly and assist you with any questions or concerns you may have.

05. What payment methods do you accept?

We accept various payment methods, including major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express), PayPal, and other secure payment options. During the checkout process, you will be able to choose the payment method that best suits your preference.
